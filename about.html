About Fish Company

Passionate about fresh, sustainable seafood

Our Story

Founded in 2010 on the shores of Mogadishu, Fish Company began as a small family venture with a single fishing boat. Today we operate a modern fleet and deliver premium seafood nationwide—while never forgetting our roots in craftsmanship, community, and care for the ocean.

Our Mission

To bring the taste of the Indian Ocean to every Somali table—fresh, fast, and responsibly harvested.

Sustainability

We follow eco-friendly fishing practices and support local fisheries to protect marine life for future generations.

Quality First

From catch to delivery, every step meets rigorous hygiene and cold-chain standards—so you enjoy seafood at its peak.

Meet the Crew

Our 120-strong team includes veteran captains, marine biologists, logistic experts, and passionate customer-service staff—all working together to guarantee your satisfaction.
